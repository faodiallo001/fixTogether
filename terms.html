1. What is FixTogether?

FixTogether is an online tool that helps two people describe a situation in writing and receive a calmer, AI-generated explanation of what happened and possible next steps. It is designed to reduce tension and help people understand each other better.

FixTogether is not therapy, not emergency support, and not legal advice.

4. What FixTogether does – and doesn’t do

FixTogether can help you:

  • Put your thoughts and feelings into words.
  • Share your side of a situation without shouting or attacking.
  • Receive a calmer message that tries to be fair to both sides.

FixTogether will not:

  • Tell you who is “right” or “wrong” in a legal sense.
  • Provide psychotherapy, medical care, or crisis support.
  • Replace a lawyer, a doctor, a mediator, or a therapist.

If you or someone else is in danger, in a violent situation, or thinking about self-harm, do not use FixTogether. Contact local emergency services or a trusted professional immediately.
