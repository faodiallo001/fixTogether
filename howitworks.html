How FixTogether works

FixTogether is a quiet, structured way to handle conflict when direct conversation feels impossible. You each write in your own time; we turn both perspectives into a calm path forward.

One conflict, four simple steps

No therapy sessions. No video calls. Just a shared written space that keeps things calm and clear.

1
You share your side
Describe what happened and how you feel — freely, without being interrupted or judged.
2
We create a private link
You send this link to the other person by WhatsApp, SMS or email. No login is needed.
3
They answer in their own words
They can read your side, then write theirs calmly. Both stories stay in one safe place.
4
You receive a neutral message
We combine both perspectives into a respectful response and concrete next steps you can accept.
