How FixTogether works

FixTogether is a calm written space for couples, friends, family members or coworkers who can’t talk without tension. Each person shares their version of what happened in their own words. The other person can read it quietly, without pressure. FixTogether then creates one balanced Fix that brings clarity, helps both sides understand each other’s feelings, and offers gentle guidance on how to move forward — whether that means reconnecting or letting go peacefully.

One conflict, four calm steps

No calls, no confrontation, no outside intervention. Just a written space where both sides can finally be heard and understood.

1
You write your version
You explain what happened and how you feel in your own words, freely and without interruption. This is your moment to speak honestly and calmly.
2
You receive a private Fix link
FixTogether generates a private link for this Fix. You share it with the other person so they can read your version safely and without emotional pressure.
3
They write their side
The other person reads your version quietly and takes their time to express their own. Both stories remain together, side by side, offering a full picture of the situation.
4
FixTogether creates the Fix
After reading both perspectives, FixTogether prepares one calm, balanced Fix that helps both sides understand each other’s feelings clearly and offers gentle guidance on how to move forward — whether that means rebuilding the connection or separating peacefully.
