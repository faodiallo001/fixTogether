How FixTogether works

FixTogether is a quiet, written space to handle conflict when direct conversation feels impossible. You each write in your own time; we read what both of you share and send you a calmer way forward by email.

One conflict, four simple steps

No therapy sessions. No video calls. Just a written space that keeps things calm, clear, and respectful for both of you.

1
You share your side
You describe what happened and how you feel — freely, without being interrupted or judged. This is your moment to put everything into words.
2
We create a private link
FixTogether creates a private link that only you can share. You send it by WhatsApp, SMS or email to the person you want to reach. No account or login is required.
3
They answer in their own words
The other person can calmly read your side, then write theirs in the same space, at their own pace. Both perspectives stay together, safely in writing.
4
We send one calm message to both of you
FixTogether reads both stories side by side and writes one neutral, balanced message that speaks to both of you at the same time. It explains what each of you is trying to say, where you’re hurting, and what could be done differently. That message — plus practical next steps — is then sent by email to you and to the other person, so you can move forward more calmly, whether that means reconnecting, setting boundaries, or ending things gently.
